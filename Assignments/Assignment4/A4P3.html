Fun

Now for some fun!

I was diagnosed with ADHD this year and in order to stay focused in class, I have to fidget with various objects. This page of the website is for you, the user, to get a taste of what I have to do to pay attention in class. Scrolling up and down on my mouse is one way that I fidget, so give it a try! As you can see, a little heart appears and disappears as you scroll. Normally, I don’t get anything out of scrolling so this makes it more fun. I also like fidgeting with my keyboard and typing really quickly. I especially like taking typing tests because they help me get some energy out when I'm feeling particularly hyperactive. On the right you’ll see a section to “yell into the void.” You can type whatever you want, and it will post it onto the site. Don’t worry, though, it’s all deleted when the page reloads so no need to worry about wasting space. Lastly, since I can’t put a stress ball onto the page – at least not without some intense coding – I have a gradient transition. Go hover your mouse over there and see it change! If it hasn’t become obvious to you yet, this site was entirely just for me to have fun, but I hope you got some enjoyment out of it too. Come back any time!
