Hi, my name is Dana!

This is a website to help you get to know me better! I'm a fourth year Bachelor of Accounting Co-op student at Brock University. In my spare time I like playing video games like Animal Crossing, Valorant, and Among Us and trying different mediums of art. I’ve dabbled in acrylic, oil, ink and watercolour paints, embroidery, crochet, knitting, and more. Basically, if you can think of an art medium, I've probably tried it. In quarantine, I added sewing to that list and made a shirt for a friend of mine. If you want to see some of the art I've created, you can go to the Art tab. I taught myself a little bit of HTML in high school, but I didn’t make it much further than changing the font colour and making headers. I’ve learned a lot this semester and this website is also to show that off! If you want to check out some fun interactive elements I've added to fidget with, you can go to the Fun tab. To access these, you can click the linked words in this introduction or use the menu button under the home icon at the top! I hope you enjoy my website and all the elements I've put in for you to interact with. Thanks for visiting!
